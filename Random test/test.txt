BLACKPINK THE MOVIE is a film to celebrate the 5th anniversary of the debut of the world-loved 
K-pop group "BLACKPINK". In this film, you can meet BLACKPINK's five years of memories sharing 
with fans all over the world, undisclosed special interviews, the most shining moments, 
and BLACKPINK's distinctive stages of "THE SHOW" (2021), "IN YOUR AREA" (2018), a dozen more hit 
songs from BLACKPINK have been re-edited and optimized for theatrical environments.
